Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often

How do I list my property on LANDLORDZS? +

Create a free account, choose the "Property Owner" role, and click "Add New Listing" from your dashboard. You'll be guided through uploading photos, entering property details, setting your price, and choosing whether the listing is for sale or rent. Listings in Yaoundé, Douala, Buea, Bamenda, Limbe, and Kribi go live immediately after a quick review by our moderation team.

How does professional verification work? +

Agents, contractors, architects, lawyers, and vendors can apply for a Verified badge through their account settings. You'll need to upload your professional licence or registration certificate, a government-issued ID, and proof of address. Our team typically reviews applications within 2–3 business days. Verified professionals receive a blue checkmark and rank higher in search results.

Does LANDLORDZS offer an escrow service? +

Yes. Our secure escrow service is available for property purchases and large rental deposits. Funds are held by our licensed financial partner until both parties confirm that all conditions of the transaction have been met. This protects buyers from fraud and gives sellers confidence that payment is guaranteed. Escrow is available for transactions in all six of our cities.

Is LANDLORDZS free to use? +

Browsing listings, searching for professionals, and creating a basic account are completely free. Property owners can post their first two listings at no cost. Agents and professionals have a free tier with limited visibility and a premium subscription that unlocks advanced analytics, priority placement, and unlimited listings. Visit our Pricing page for full details.

How do I report a suspicious or fraudulent listing? +

Every listing has a "Report this listing" button. Click it, select the reason (e.g. suspected fraud, incorrect information, already sold/rented), and add any details that might help our team investigate. You can also email us directly at report@landlordzs.cm or use the contact form above and select "Report a Listing" as the subject. We investigate all reports within 48 hours.
