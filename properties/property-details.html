Property Description

This exceptional 4-bedroom villa is nestled in the prestigious Bastos neighbourhood of Yaoundé, widely regarded as the city's most secure and diplomatic quarter. Set within a fully gated and guarded community, the property offers unobstructed views of the Yaoundé hills and a serene, private atmosphere rarely found in the capital.

Finished to an exacting modern standard, the villa features an open-plan living and dining area flooded with natural light, a fully fitted kitchen with granite worktops, and four spacious en-suite bedrooms with built-in wardrobes. The location is unrivalled: just minutes from the US Embassy, the French Embassy, SGBC Bank, the International School of Yaoundé, and Bastos market. 24/7 water supply and electricity are guaranteed through dedicated water storage tanks and a high-capacity standby generator. This is a rare opportunity to acquire a true turnkey luxury residence in the heart of Yaoundé's embassy district.
